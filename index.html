How it works

Four swift steps to spend less time waiting and more time eating.

01

Discover

Browse curated picks or search thousands of NYC spots. See what’s buzzing now.

02

Check live waits

Compare real‑time waits on a city map. Color‑coded pins show what’s short, medium, or long.

03

Set alerts

Pick a place and get notified when the wait drops below your threshold.

04

Go now

Head out when the line is light. Directions and booking links are one tap away.
